    Lesson 1: <Say>


    The <Say> verb converts text into speech that will be played in your call. Try it out!

    voice
    The voice attribute controls whether the text is read with a male voice or a female voice.
    Attribute NameAllowed ValuesDefault Values
    voiceman, womanman
    language
    The language attribute controls what language’s accent and pronunciations will be used to read the text.
    Attribute NameAllowed ValuesDefault Values
    languageen, en-gb, es, fr, deen
