    What is TwiML?

    TwiML is the set of instructions that Twilio uses.

    When someone makes a call or sends an SMS to a Twilio number, Twilio will look up TwiML associated with that number and use it to determine what to do. For example, the TwiML could tell Twilio to record the call, play a message, or prompt the caller to press digits on their keypad.
